$$\boldsymbol{u}_t + \nabla \cdot \boldsymbol{f}_a(\boldsymbol{u}) + A(\boldsymbol{u}) \nabla \boldsymbol{u} = \nabla \cdot \boldsymbol{f}_v(\boldsymbol{u}, \nabla \boldsymbol{u}) + \boldsymbol{s}(\boldsymbol{u})$$
